What can you do here?

Dissect is your creative portfolio's living DNA. You explore it like a map — not like a dashboard. Here's how.

Hover a star
Quick-look: thumbnail + tagline + key metrics. Zero commitment.
Click a star
Deep-dive card: full AI analysis, tagline, hook breakdown, insights, script thesis. Click elsewhere to close.
Drag a star
Move it anywhere. It stays pinned. Put two creatives side-by-side to compare visually.
Lasso L
Press L, then drag across any region. Get instant cohort stats: what attributes they share, how they lift metric.
Chat on the right
Chat is a filter scalpel. Type in natural language; it reshapes the graph in real-time via tool calls.
Pin an insight
Whenever you spot a pattern, pin it. Pinned findings become building blocks for your next report.
Attribute clusters
Big colored labels (HOOK STYLE, CLAIMS, etc.) mark attribute families. Creatives gravitate toward the attributes they carry.
Metric glow
Switch the metric (ROAS, spend, ECR) and every star re-colors in real time. Green = top quartile on that metric.
