What can you do here?

Dissect is your creative portfolio's living DNA. Five input vocabularies, one constellation.

Hover
Quick-peek. Thumbnail + tagline + metrics. Zero commitment.
Click
Deep-dive card with 10+ collapsible sections: hook, script, pacing, payoff/CTA, audio, visual, sentiment, claims, peers, siblings.
Drag
Pin a creative anywhere — or drop it on Bay A / Bay B to build a comparison.
Lasso L
Draw around a region. Instant cohort stats. Hold ⇧ to add to existing selection.
Walk W
Click-walk from one creative to its neighbors. Each step adds to a breadcrumb that becomes a saveable story.
Time scrubber
Drag the bottom slider to weight toward 2025 or 2026. Creatives fade proportionally.
Bays A vs B
Drop creatives into each bay. Once both have 1+, a delta panel slides in with metric + attribute comparisons.
Chat scalpel
Type in natural language; Dissect translates to tool calls and reshapes the canvas.
